    • Updates for GPRD - The EU's General Data Protection Regulation

      What is the GDPR?
      As most of you already know, the new EU GDPR will be enforced on May 25th, 2018. The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (EU) 2016/679 is a regulation in EU law on data protection and privacy for all individuals within the European Union. It addresses the export of personal data outside the EU. The GDPR aims primarily to give control back to citizens and residents over their personal data and to simplify the regulatory environment for international business by unifying the regulation within the EU.

      Source: Wikipedia.org
